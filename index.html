About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Kennesaw State University, working as a Graduate Research Assistant under Dr. Honghui Xu. Previously, I completed a B.E. in Electronics, Communication and Information Engineering from Tribhuvan University, IOE Pashchimanchal Campus, advised by Asst. Prof. Smita Adhikari.

I build end-to-end embedded and edge systems, from data collection and firmware to model training and deployment, with a focus on secure and privacy-preserving AI on resource-constrained devices.
